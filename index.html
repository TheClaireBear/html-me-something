Claire's Favorite dishes to eat

Food is what brings my family together and has always been a big part of my life. These are just a few of my favorite comfort food.

Mac&Cheese

My first food love. This is what i would always have my dad make me on my birthday. Over the years i have made some interestig variations of the original dish.

Drunken Noodles

This is the perfect hang-over dish. The harty flavors of the sauce, noodles, and veggies really fill you up. Gets you in the perfect state to sleep the rest of the hang-over away.

Seasonal Salad

I'm usually not a fruit-in-salad person, but this salad is on a whole different level. It is from Teaspoon Cafe in Edwardsville where they make their own poppy seed dressing that goes on this salad. It will make you re-think your life decisions on food.
